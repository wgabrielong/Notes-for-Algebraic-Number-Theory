\section*{Preliminaries}
These notes roughly correspond to the course \textbf{MATH 223A: Algebraic Number Theory} taught by Prof. Melanie Wood at Harvard University in the Fall 2023 semester.\footnote{I switched to auditing the class in the midst of the semester, and was unable to make it to a number of class sessions.} These notes are \LaTeX-ed after the fact with significant alteration and are subject to misinterpretation and mistranscription. Use with caution. Any errors are undoubtedly my own and any virtues that could be ascribed to these notes ought be attributed to the instructor and not the typist. A special thanks goes to Matt King, the official note taker for the course, for his notes have greatly improved my own.
